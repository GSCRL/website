Can I volunteer?

If you'd like to help us put on an event, shoot us an email at njcombatrobots@gmail.com and tell us that you're interested in volunteering. Having an event in mind can help us make sure that you get assigned and can learn the ropes as we grow the league!

Additionally, join our Discord and talk to us! We're a rag-tag crew but we're always happy to chat about the league if you'd prefer to chat there.

Can I help host an event?

If you or your employer is interested in hosting the league to run an event, please send us us an email with njcombatrobots@gmail.com.

Not all spaces or event insurances are viable unfortunately, but we're happy to talk it over and try to work something out.

How do I give you money?

Donations can be made to FUBAR Labs, New Jersey's oldest hackerspace.

They've offered to do passthrough for us while we focus on running high-quality events.

Additionally, FUBAR Labs a registered charity, so donations made to us through them are tax deductable. Many of FUBAR Labs' members have been essential in the league's operation from the beginning, and we thank them for their continued support!
